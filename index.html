Many professionals may not have the time to answer questions, and other amateurs may not have the experience or the answers. That's where we come in. Meet the Founders

Joshua is a dedicated, classically trained musician and has played the piano for over 10 years. He has since gained an interest in programming and often wonders about the intersection between music, physics, and computer science. While at MIT, Joshua hopes to study and conduct research in machine learning, music, and quantum computing.
