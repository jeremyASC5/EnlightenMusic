About Us

Often times, budding musicians need advice and insight on certain topics‚Äìfast. Many professionals may not have the time to answer questions, and other amateurs may not have the experience or the answers. That's where we come in. EnlightenMusic is a web app dedicated to helping these musicians build ideas off of each other to improve their craft and improve their music. No registration is required; simply write your name with every post and type away!
